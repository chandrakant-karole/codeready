APP Features

  • 1
    Florist's availability toggle

    The registered florists can make their availability set via this feature. This assists the customer to decide and place their orders accordingly.

  • 2
    Florist Profile

    This feature shows florists information including their years of experience, flower categories served, visiting/service fees and much more with ease.

  • 3
    Schedule Appointment

    Service seekers can schedule an appointment with the nearest florist for any flower related services. The feature allows easy cancellation as well.

  • 4
    Push Notifications

    Users will be notified with delivery updates, offers, and other information via push notifications, email, SMS and other means as per their discretion.

  • 5
    Payment Integration

    The app will comprise numerous popular payment gateways coupled with multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and others.

  • 6
    Geolocation

    With this special feature, customers can find the nearest florists and similarly delivery experts can locate the delivery point with ease.

  • 7
    Search By specialist

    Based on requirements, service seekers can make extensive use of the formidable search engine and find the local florist that best suit their needs.

  • 1
    God's Eye View

    Verified Providers

    Admin will verify the florist's details and mark them as verified for the customers to choose and order from.

    Bill Estimation

    The feature displays the final price to be paid by the customer before checkout.

  • 4
    OTP Verification

    The service provider and the customer alike gets authenticated by our apps. An SMS notification containing the OTP will be sent to their registered number which in turn eliminates the fake users and requests.

    Advanced Analytics

    This powerful admin feature provides a complete bird's eye view of customer's and florist's activities including service requests, bookings, deliveries, commissions, locations and much more.

    Advanced Reports

    With advanced reports, you can check out where the areas that need more marketing to increase your commission. It also gives you a clear-cut idea of planning your business.

  • 7
    Accept/ Reject Request

    Based on their availability, florists can accept or reject flower orders. The sophisticated algorithm connects customers with the available local florists instantly.

    Multi-language

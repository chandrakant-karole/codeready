How does it work?

With your well-crafted employee transportation management system, enhance your business to global expectations.

Ride request

Employees book a ride via their company account details and cancel the ride if there are any changes in the plan.

Auto dispatch

The ride request is fulfilled via the auto dispatch mode. The concerned driver will be assigned for the trip.

Monitor

The dispatch manager can monitor and track all the corporate trips through the cutting-edge central dashboard.

Features

Employee App

Direction & timing

This feature shows details like approved routes, timings, stops and much more for employees.
Real-time tracking

View and track assigned vehicle location on a map coupled with precise ETA information.
Ad-hoc requests

The feature allows the app to cater to unplanned trip requests by employees anytime with ease.

Ride check-in

For a secure and transparent trip, employees can verify the ride with the advanced QR code based check-in.
Trip details

Easily view trip details including driver profile, vehicle number, pick & drop points and others.

Notifications

Update employees on the upcoming trip with timely alerts via push notifications, SMS, and email.
SOS button

During any unforeseen emergency, employees can raise the alarm for speedy assistance anytime.
Ratings and reviews

After each trip, employees can rate their ride experience and also provide additional feedback for the same.
Help & support

The feature allows employees to connect directly with the assigned corporate transport help desk.

This feature allows drivers to view all the upcoming assigned trips with details such as time of pickup, location, and others.
Pickup & drop details

Drivers can view the list of employee details as well as their pickup & drop location information. This also doubles up as a quasi-attendance register.
Navigation

Drivers can pick up and drop the employees easily with seamless navigation available on the app.

SOS button

Track earnings

Notifications

Update employees on the upcoming trip with timely alerts via push notifications, SMS, and email.

Admin/Transportation manager

Request management

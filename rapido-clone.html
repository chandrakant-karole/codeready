How Does The Rapido Clone Script Work?

Step 1: Request for a Ride
A passenger uses the Rapido clone app to request a ride. The request is forwarded to all nearby drivers.
Step 2: Acceptance of the Ride
The request is accepted by the driver. The passenger's pick-up and drop-off locations are then given to the driver.
Step 3: Pick it up
The driver arrives at the passenger's location and uses a unique PIN to verify their identification. The ride will then begin.
Step 4: Drop
The passenger can use the app to trace their trip in real time, and the ride is considered complete once they arrive at their destination.
Step 5: Payment
In the Rapido clone, the passenger is prompted with fare information. They can pay the driver personally or use their card or make a digital payment within the app.

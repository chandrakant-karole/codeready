Software Features

Our solutions inherit the best features that focus on scalability and performance to make your platform stand out from the crowd.

Price Calculator

Customers can view the pricing for different routes when planning their travel budget.

Order Customization

Customers can customize their ride by availing extra add-ons such as additional beverages, food, WiFi and more.

Instant Matching

Customers will be liked to drivers almost instantly. This helps them save time and enjoy your services faster.

Real-time Tracking

Customers can track the status and whereabouts of the dispatched limousine in real-time.

Simplified Navigation

The driver will receive the pick/drop locations of the passengers as well as an optimized route to reach them quickly.

Invoice Management

The platform automatically generates a detailed invoice that contains details of the routes and other services availed by the passenger.

Fleet Management

The admin can accurately track the status and location of dispatched and vacant limousines in real-time.

Payroll Processing

The system calculates the driver's earnings in a precise manner, and the admin can disburse wages instantly.

Surge Pricing

The admin can momentarily increase the fare prices on the platform to mirror the increase in demand.
